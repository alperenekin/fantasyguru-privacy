FantasyGuru Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 20, 2025

FantasyGuru (“we”, “our”, “us”) operates a fantasy basketball companion app that integrates with Yahoo Fantasy Sports. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application (“App”) and related backend services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information:

  • Information from Yahoo Fantasy Sports: When you connect your Yahoo account through OAuth, we receive data such as your Yahoo Fantasy Sports profile ID, your fantasy basketball league, team, and roster information. This data is retrieved using Yahoo’s official Fantasy Sports API (via the yfpy library). We do not have permission to modify or delete any Yahoo data — only to read it.
  • Cached Data: We temporarily cache your league-related data on our secure backend server to improve app performance and reduce API load. This cached data is automatically cleared after a short period and is not permanently stored.
  • Crash and Performance Data: We use Firebase Crashlytics to identify and fix app stability issues. Firebase may collect device identifiers, crash logs, and technical information.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Display your Yahoo Fantasy Basketball data within the app
  • Provide analysis, insights, and recommendations related to your fantasy teams
  • Maintain and improve the app’s reliability and performance

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

3. Data Sharing

Your data is only shared with:

  • Yahoo Fantasy Sports API – to retrieve your league information
  • Firebase Crashlytics – for crash diagnostics

4. Data Security

All communication between the app, our backend, and Yahoo’s API occurs over HTTPS. Cached data is stored securely and automatically deleted after use.

5. Data Retention

Cached Yahoo data is retained only temporarily. Once the session expires or cache is cleared, this data is deleted from our servers.

You can revoke our app’s access to your Yahoo account at any time via your Yahoo account settings.

6. Your Rights

  • Revoke Yahoo access at any time
  • Request deletion of cached data by emailing alperenekin97@gmail.com
  • Request more information about how your data is used

7. Children’s Privacy

FantasyGuru is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect or store data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or requests regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at alperenekin97@gmail.com.
